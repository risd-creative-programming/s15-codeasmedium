Midterm: DUE 3/30

Present a concept sketch, and explain how you envision it developing over the next 6 weeks for the final project. You will have 15 minutes for presentation+feedback, a guest critic will be present to help ask questions. It could be installation, I have green room booked, and room 305 annexes.

Final project:
  • A project that represents 6 (well really 13) weeks of thoughtful work, a process.
  • Something that fits into your practice and the larger ideas you’re working with right now.
  • This should not be just a technical demo, I am *much* more interested in code used in a deliberate, considered way that opens new creative potential for your work. It need not be computer based, but should use programming principles. Think beyond the capabilities of this library, you can use other JS libraries, Java Processing, other??
  • Consideration (1) relationship of programming to your own work. — How does it allow you to extend? What are the implications of using programming in the way you are choosing? What is unique about this form/medium for you?
  • Consideration (2) precedents — What has been done already? What areas have not been explored? How are you innovating?
  • Consideration (3) audience — Who is your desired audience? How will this piece reach them? What is the optimal form? We will work out later as a class best way to view everyone’s work.
