Assignment 2: DUE 3/2

  • Create a composition further exploring the themes you were showed in class that reacts to the mouse by transforming between two different states or perspectives, both visually and conceptually. That is, your viewer should feel like they are seeing things one way, then upon moving the mouse, arrive at or be confronted with an unexpectedly different perspective. Email the link to a page running your code by next Sunday at 5pm. Some examples to ponder:

    • Mary Huang, Typeface
    • Caitlin Berrigan and Anya Liftig, Adoring Appetite
    • Tim Noble and Sue Webster
    • Matthew Mazzotta, Insertion Module
    • Matthew Mazzotta, Looking for a Landscape
    • The Yes Men
  • Watch The Queen of Code video (~17min).
  • Read Digital Divide by Claire Bishop.
